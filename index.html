Baofeng Chang (常宝峰)

I'm a Ph.D. undergraduate in ZJUTVis Lab, Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University of Technology, Hangzhou, China, where I'm working with Associate Professor Guodao Sun and Professor Ronghua Liang.

My research interest mainly lies on Dynamic Graph Visualization, Temporal Sequence Visualization, and Traffic Visualization. I'm experienced in development of visual analysis prototype system. My research scenarios involves sharing bike, music, sports, etc.

I received bachelor's degree in Automation from College of Information Engineering, Zhejiang University of Technology in 2017. Now, I'm doing with doctoral degree in computer science and technology. I'm looking for collaboration and interchange in visual analysis.
